the captain of the Donquixote Pirates, the king of Dressrosa and a member of the Shichibukai before his defeat by Luffy and arrest by the Marine Admiral Fujitora. Formerly the most influential underworld broker, Doflamingo's defeat and capture sent shockwaves throughout the New World, enraging his affiliates, including one of the Four Emperors, Kaido. He is the arch-nemesis of Trafalgar D. Water Law, the Riku Royal Family, and the Tontatta Kingdom.
